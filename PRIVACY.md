# Privacy Policy — Brainrot3d

_Last updated: 2026-06-29_

Brainrot3d is an independent, on-device client. It has no backend of its own.

## What the app stores
- **Your login session** (an Instagram bearer token and related device identifiers) is stored
  **only on your device**, in the app's local storage, so you don't have to log in every time.
  It is never sent anywhere except to Instagram's own servers, as part of normal API calls you
  initiate.
- The app does **not** contain any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

## What the app sends, and to whom
- The app communicates **directly with Instagram's servers** (`i.instagram.com` and Instagram's
  media CDNs) to log in, load your feed, and perform actions you take (like, comment, share).
- An optional **"Algorithm signals"** switch, **off by default**, controls whether a normal
  "watched this reel" signal is reported to Instagram. With it off, nothing is reported.

## What the developer receives
- **Nothing.** There is no server operated by this project. The developer does not receive your
  credentials, your activity, or any usage data.

## Deleting your data
- Log out in the app, or delete the app, to remove the stored session from your device.

## Disclaimer
This project is not affiliated with, authorized by, or endorsed by Instagram or Meta. Use it
only with your own account and at your own risk.
